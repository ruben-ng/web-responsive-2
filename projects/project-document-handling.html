Document Handling

the project involved handling the documentation for processing a jetty special terminal permit for PT. Caterpillar. The process began with gathering all necessary information and requirements for the permit application. This included preparing detailed documentation, such as technical specifications, safety protocols, and compliance reports. Coordination with regulatory bodies and stakeholders was crucial to ensure all legal and procedural requirements were met. The application was meticulously completed and submitted, followed by ongoing communication to address any queries or additional requirements from the authorities. Once the permit was approved, all documentation was finalized and archived. The project concluded with a review to ensure all processes were handled efficiently and to identify any improvements for future permit applications.
