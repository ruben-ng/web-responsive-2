Shipment

the logistics project involved shipping galvanized bamboo wooden beams from India to Batam, and then from Batam to Bogor for PT Ecomat Pro. The process began with detailed planning and scheduling, which included assessing the shipment requirements and coordinating with all relevant parties. Preparation involved securely packaging the bamboo beams, labeling them for easy identification, and ensuring all necessary documentation and permits were in place for international and domestic transport. The shipment was first transported from India to Batam, selecting appropriate shipping methods and managing transit logistics. Upon arrival in Batam, the bamboo beams were then re-shipped to Bogor. During both legs of the journey, careful monitoring was conducted to ensure the beams arrived in good condition and on schedule. The project concluded with unpacking and inspecting the shipment in Bogor, finalizing all related documentation, and reviewing the process to ensure a successful operation and address any issues.
