About us

Who we are

Customer satisfaction is our top most priority

Established in 2019, Yusra Transindo Service is a Batam-based, dedicated freight forwarding company with a global outlook. Yusra Transindo Service prides itself in being a comprehensive one-stop shipping solutions provider – offering an extensive and innovative suite of services including freight forwarding, chartering, trans-shipment, pick and pack, project logistics and more. We believe in being part of a value added supply chain to achieve total cost savings for companies.

Vision

Yusra boasts the ability to cater to different clients’ needs from planning the optimal system of freight transportation right through to operation execution, thereby giving our clients an unsurpassed advantage and cost savings in today’s competitive environment.

Mission

Customer satisfaction is our top most priority, and the driving force behind our operations. Your Personal Yusra, Consultant provides a single point of contact so you are assured of service excellence from the team, every step of the way.
